THE LOST FORREST

Relax and enjoy a picnic with your family and friends in this shady picnic spot by the edge of the lake. The Lost Forrest is a great place to take a break from the hustle and bustle of the zoo. It is a peaceful area with lots of trees and a beautiful view of the lake. Picnic tables and clean bathrooms are provided.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forrest is to the east of the zoo, beyond Monkey Trail.
