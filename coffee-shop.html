COFFEE SHOP

Come and relax with a fragrant, freshly-brewed cuppa at Wild Things Coffee Shop. Enjoy the view of the zoo's lush greenery while you sip your coffee. The coffee shop is a great place to take a break and recharge during your visit. They serve a variety of coffees, teas, and cold beverages, all made with high-quality ingredients. They also serve a variety of sandwiches, confectionery and desserts, in case you're peckish!
